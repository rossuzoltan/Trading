# AGENTS.md

## Mission
Increase the probability of robust out-of-sample, post-cost profitability.
Do not optimize for attractive but fragile backtests.

## Hard rules
- Work in `C:\dev\trading-dev`, never in the active training workspace.
- Do not re-audit the live/runtime architecture unless a failing test proves it is required.
- Fix evaluation/accounting before adding new RL complexity.
- Compare RL against simple baselines under the exact same cost model.
- Treat execution diagnostics and audited economic metrics as more important than internal PPO health.
- Do not declare success from in-sample or single-split gains.
- Prefer simpler systems when evidence for RL is weak.

## Priority order
1. Evaluation/reporting correctness
2. Leakage and cost-model correctness
3. Baseline suite
4. Churn/cost diagnostics
5. Reward/action redesign
6. Walk-forward robustness

## Required validation
- `.\.venv\Scripts\python.exe -m unittest discover tests`
- symbol-specific evaluation
- comparable baseline report
- markdown summary of what changed and what remains unproven
